Where can I download a copy of the updated class schedule?

If you are enrolled in Lithan Academy's classes, you can download log onto Teamie with your username and password. Once you are logged onto Teamie, click on Modules and the course that you are enrolled in. The updated class schedules is in the announcement post.

What is Victoria University's online student services?

MYVU Portal is your gateway to Victoria University's online student services. It allows you to:

  • View your enrolment details
  • Apply to graduate
  • Access STUDENT CONNECT, VU Collaborate
  • Download and print certified Statement of Results
  • Access other important tools and information.

How do I log onto Victoria University (Australia) Portal?

Access Victoria University (Australia) online student services through MYVU portal. Username: enter "s" in lowercase followed by your student ID number, e.g. s4534150. Password: If you are a first time user, your password will be by default, "Temp" (uppercase T) followed by your date of birth (Tempddmmyyyy). E.g. if your date of birth is: 12th May 1983, your default password will be Temp12051983. Once you log into the portal, you will be prompted to change your temporary password.

I have problems logging into the portal, who should I contact? 

If you have problems logging into Blackboard or Student Portal, please email to VU ITS Service Desk (servicedesk@vu.edu.au) indicating the following:
VU Student ID :
Full Name :
Address :
Date of Birth :
Name of course enrolled:
Campus Location : (Lithan Academy Singapore)
